🎙 LIVE LISTENING
Synko can listen to your meeting in real time and automatically detect treats — no pasting required. It captures tab audio (everyone's voice) and uses Deepgram for speech-to-text with speaker identification.
Free tier: 45 hrs/month. Get a key at deepgram.com → Console → API Keys. Without this key, only microphone capture (your voice only) is available.
AI ANALYSIS
Synko uses Claude AI to analyze transcript chunks and detect communication moments. Without a key, basic keyword detection is used.
Get a key at console.anthropic.com.

How should the pet behave when it spots a vague or unclear message in Slack? Quiet pet stays out of your way until you click. Active pet pops the suggestion the moment it notices.
